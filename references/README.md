- [standards-and-regulations.md](standards-and-regulations.md) -- frameworks and statutes with scope

- [glossary.md](glossary.md) -- AppSec / DevSecOps terms

## Curation policy

- Primary sources: canonical specifications (RFC, NIST, ISO), vendor
  documentation, and books with a verifiable author.
- Supporting sources: well-known research blogs and conference talks
  with a stable URL.
- Skip entries that cannot be cited with a verifiable name and URL.
  Mark uncertain entries with `<!-- TODO: verify -->` rather than
  guessing.
